Established in 2000, Downeast Logistics is a CVE certified Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), Economically Disadvantaged Women Owned Small Business (EDWOSB), and DOT Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE/WBE). Downeast Logistics’ success is reflected through continuously high Dun & Bradstreet and DOD Supplier Performance Rating Systems scores. A testament to almost two decades of contracts for the Defense Logistics Agency, Military Services, Department of Veterans Affairs, Federal Aviation Administration, and many others.

Built upon the core principles of efficiency, reliability, and continuous improvement, Downeast Logistics offers the added values of experience and proven customer service. All fulfilled with safety, security, and environmental requirements in mind. Downeast Logistics provides dependable answers for acquisition.
